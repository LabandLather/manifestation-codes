❓ How to use

Getting started
Tap Medical or Life to open a section and start entering conditions and codes.

Sort A–Z
Tap ↑↓ A–Z to sort alphabetically. The code always stays linked to its condition.

Translate
Tap 🌍 Translate to switch the entire app to another language. Conditions and all buttons translate. Always starts in English.

Select
Tap ☑ Select, then tap rows to select them. Use ☑ All to select everything. Print or save your selection.

Print
Tap 🖨 Print for a clean A4 printout. Buttons and row numbers are hidden.

Save / Share
Tap 📤 Save to open the iPhone share sheet — choose Drive, AirDrop, Files, or any app.

Email
Tap 📧 Mail to copy your data to the clipboard, then paste it into any email app.
💡 Tip: for a neat layout, print to PDF first and attach that to your email.

Load
Use Load on the home screen to restore a previously saved .json file.
